Online Excel CPD, Provided by The Excel Club

When you select this option you will be brought to the Excel Club ATI Platform. Your Excel Club log in details will be sent to you by ATI in a separate email


Step 1: Select Online Excel CPD, Provided by The Excel Club


Step 2: Select Log In. Log in using the email address you have registered in your Touchpoint Account and the password sent to you by ATI in a separate email


Step 3: Open your profile, select Sign in and Security and reset your password


Step 3: RETAIN YOUR LOG IN DETAILS


Step 4: On the home screen you will see the full Online Excel offering including 2 FREE One Hour Excel courses and a wide selection of 2 hour and longer courses. You are entitled to complete any one of the 2 hour courses FREE

Step 5: Complete the Guide for ATI Members


Step 6: When you have decided what 2 hour course you want to complete FREE please email cpd@accountingtechniciansireland.ie and you will be given the access


Step 7: If you wish to complete any further online Excel training you can purchase it direct via this platform
